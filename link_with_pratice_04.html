Dragon ball-Z

Dragon Ball Z is a Japanese anime television series produced by Toei Animation. It is the sequel to the original Dragon Ball series and follows the adventures of Goku, his friends, and their battles against powerful foes. The series is known for its intense action, memorable characters, and iconic transformations like Super Saiyan. It has become a cultural phenomenon worldwide, inspiring numerous adaptations, merchandise, and a dedicated fanbase.

In the original Dragon Ball, Goku was far stronger than nearly every opponent he faced. This was often played for comedy, with plenty of jokes being made about the small monkey boy easily winning all his fights, but it still means that Goku was rarely challenged in fights during his early adventures.

With exceptions like his battles against Tien Shinhan, King Piccolo, and Piccolo Jr, nearly all of Goku's fights in Dragon Ball were downright unfair. Unfortunately for his friends Krillin and Yamcha, their fights were equally unfair, but typically not in their favor.
